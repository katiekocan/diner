WELCOMES YOU!

“Our mission is to promote sustainability and reduce our carbon footprint. We achieve this by sourcing our ingredients from local farmers who use sustainable farming practices. We also grow our own herbs and vegetables in our garden, which reduces our reliance on outside suppliers. Additionally, we use compostable packaging and utensils and recycle our waste. We happily provide dairy-free, gluten-free, and vegan options for our customers to enjoy ethical American cuisine.”

Debbie Williams

Our Marvelous Menu

Bountiful Burger

A delicious, plant-based burger served with locally grown lettuce and tomato served on a homemade gluten-free bun.

Planted Pizza

Plant-based cheddar cheese and locally grown tomato sauce on homemade gluten-free garlic-herb crust, sprinkled with vegan parmesan cheese.

Chicken Tenders

Crispy, crunchy, vegan chicken tenders served with a side of roasted potato fries.

Roasted Potato Fries

A plethora of home grown potato wedges, fried in olive oil. Available as a side or as a basket.

Mac 'n Cheese

Homemade macaroni noodles drowned in plant-based cheddar cheese. Availble as a side or full bowl.

Apple Pie

Homemade apple pie made with locally grown honeycrisp apples and gluten-free crust. Can be served with a scoop of homemade, dairy-free ice cream.
